Web & Application Development Engineer

Web and Application development careers are in high demand. According to the U.S. Department of Labor the job market for software developers is forecasted to grow much faster than the average for other occupations. The fast growth in web applications and mobile computing combined with the increasing use of software continues to create strong demand for software developers. Florida Vocational Institute offers a program in web and application development that is designed for someone with no programing experience to learn how to develop websites and web based applications in less than 10 months.


The course will teach you how to develop web sites and use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as well as popular frameworks like jQuery and React.JS. Once you master these skills you will learn the fundamentals of database technologies. You will also have an opportunity to learn how to develop more exciting server side applications using php and Node.JS. Career opportunities in web development are extensive. The Bureau of Labor Statistics expects web development careers to grow at 20% between 2012 and 2022. FVI maintains strong working relationships with top companies in the area so we can match your passion and ability to career opportunities.

Web Design Foundations

At the very beginning of the track, all our students learn the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Front end Web Development

Once foundational HTML skills have been established, the remainder of the first half of our program teaches students the basics of jquery, interacting with API's, developing single page applications, and querying databases.

Back End Development

The latter half of our track is devoted to teaching you the foundations of php and node.JS development. Our students become full stack web developers in two languages.

Founded in February 2007, Florida Vocational Institute's purpose is to offer affordable training and employable skills in challenging and rewarding careers. Our mission is to train students to become entry level professionals in high demand careers. We aim to improve employability and inspire life long career growth, thereby improving the life quality of individuals in our community.

Why Choose us

At Florida Vocational Institute, job-readiness comes first.

  • Project-based Learning

    We have a pragmatic, hands-on approach to learning. We want you to be the best developer you can be, and our curriculum is hyperfocused on web development skills.

  • Small Classes

    Our small classes allow our students to learn on a mentorship based model. You will work one on one with industry pros who have ample experience developing real applications.

  • Modern Frameworks

    Our curriculum focuses on frameworks that are widely in demand in the job market. We don't care about the flavor of the month in Javascript frameworks, we care about teaching our students the skills that will get them employed by established companies.
